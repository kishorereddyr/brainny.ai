Our Story

As a parent, I experienced the quiet uncertainty that comes with supporting a child’s learning without clear insight into their strengths and challenges. Despite the dedication of teachers and tutors, the realities of large classes and limited time often meant that targeted guidance was an exception rather than the rule.

Observing my own children, I noticed how collaboration with peers sparked engagement and accelerated understanding. Conversations with other parents and educators confirmed a shared need: a reliable, data-informed way to identify each learner’s precise needs while preserving the human connection that motivates growth.

This insight led to BRAINNY—an AI study companion designed to illuminate individual learning paths for students & provide parents and teachers with secure, actionable feedback. Together, we can ensure every learner receives the specific support required to thrive.
